Why Study DRAM?

📊

Performance Engineering

Cache misses that fall through to DRAM are the single biggest bottleneck in most modern software. Understanding DRAM lets you write code that respects memory latency and bandwidth limits.

🛠

Hardware Design

SoC, FPGA, and PCB designers must pick the right DRAM type, configure controllers, and meet signal-integrity requirements. This knowledge is prerequisite.

🔒

Security Research

Rowhammer and its descendants (TRRespass, Blacksmith, RowPress) are some of the most impactful hardware vulnerabilities of the last decade — all rooted in DRAM physics.

🌐

Industry Relevance

DRAM is a $100B+ market. Understanding its roadmap — DDR5, LPDDR5X, HBM3, CXL Memory — is essential for product, silicon, and systems roles at any major tech company.
